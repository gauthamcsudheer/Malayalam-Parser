OTHERS

In addition to the commonly known parts of speech like nouns, verbs, and adjectives, there are several other important categories. Circumpositions are adpositions that can appear before and after their complements. Disjunctions, on the other hand, indicate choices or alternatives. Echo words repeat or emphasize parts of a sentence, while multiword expressions are groups of words with a single meaning. Each of these categories plays a unique role in language, contributing to its richness and complexity.
